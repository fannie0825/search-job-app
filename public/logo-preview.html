Design Rationale

The CareerLens logo features a "Focus Aperture" design that plays on the word "Lens." It uses a stylized camera aperture or iris formed into the shape of a "C" to represent:

  • Clarity: Clear focus on the right opportunities
  • Precision: AI-powered targeting and matching
  • Vision: Seeing the right career path
  • Technology: Modern, tech-forward aesthetic suitable for fintech/corporate environments
